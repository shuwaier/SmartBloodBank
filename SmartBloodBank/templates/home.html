Who we are...

The goal is to assist in serving donors and patient who need blood in the easiest, fastest and convenient way.

The system can show the distribution of camps in various part of the country and provide locations of the donation camps and allow donors to contact(Email) with these donations camps for more information and questions that need to be answered, and allow to leader of Campaign to add a new donation camp to the list of the donation camps that the system have so that it would be available for all donors to see.

Advantages of Blood Donation

  • Refreshing the blood system
  • Stimulating the bone marrow to produce new red blood cells, white blood cells and platelet
  • Reducing the risk of cardiovascular diseases
  • Getting rid of excess iron accumulated in our body which may lead to hemochromatosis.
